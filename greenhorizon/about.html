About Green Horizon

Pioneering sustainable solutions through innovation, education, and community action

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Green Horizon began as a small team of environmental enthusiasts determined to bridge the gap between sustainability knowledge and action. Today, we've grown into a global platform empowering individuals and businesses to make meaningful environmental changes.

What started as a simple carbon calculator has evolved into a comprehensive ecosystem of tools, resources, and community support designed to make sustainable living accessible to everyone.

Our Approach

We believe sustainability should be simple, rewarding, and data-driven. Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with behavioral science to create tools that inspire real change.

By focusing on measurable impact, personalized recommendations, and community support, we help users turn environmental intentions into lasting habits.
