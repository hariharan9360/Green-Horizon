Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our sustainability tools and resources

General Questions

What is Green Horizon's mission?

Green Horizon empowers individuals and communities to adopt sustainable lifestyles through actionable insights, innovative tools, and community support. Our mission is to make environmental responsibility accessible, measurable, and rewarding for everyone.

How does Green Horizon calculate carbon footprints?

Our carbon calculator uses scientifically validated emission factors from trusted sources like the EPA and IPCC. We analyze your energy usage, transportation habits, diet, and consumption patterns to estimate your personal carbon footprint with industry-standard methodologies.

Is Green Horizon free to use?

Yes! Our core platform including the carbon calculator, eco challenges, and community features are completely free. We offer premium subscriptions for advanced analytics and personalized coaching, but our mission is to make sustainability accessible to all.

Carbon Footprint Tool

How accurate is the carbon footprint calculator?

Our calculator provides estimates within 10-15% accuracy for most users. For precise measurements, we recommend connecting your utility accounts directly. Remember that all footprint calculations are estimates - what matters most is tracking your relative progress over time.

Can I track my footprint over time?

Absolutely! When you create an account, we automatically save your calculations and generate progress reports. Our dashboard visualizes your monthly trends, highlights reduction achievements, and suggests personalized improvement areas.

What's the best way to reduce my carbon footprint?

Our data shows the most impactful actions are: 1) Switching to renewable energy, 2) Reducing air travel, 3) Adopting a plant-based diet, and 4) Improving home energy efficiency. Based on your specific results, we'll create a personalized action plan with achievable steps.

Eco Challenges

How do the eco challenges work?

Our challenges are 30-day programs that guide you through sustainable habit formation. Each day you'll receive a small, achievable task with educational resources. Complete tasks to earn points, unlock badges, and see your environmental impact grow in real-time.

Can I create custom challenges?

Yes! Premium members can design personalized challenges tailored to their specific goals. Our challenge builder lets you select from hundreds of actions or create completely custom tasks with their own environmental impact metrics.

Are there community challenges I can join?

We host monthly community-wide challenges where thousands of participants work toward collective goals. These often have themes like "Plastic-Free July" or "Energy Conservation Month." You'll see your contribution to the group's total impact.

Still have questions?

Our sustainability experts are happy to help with any additional questions you may have about Green Horizon or your environmental impact journey.
